Forecasting Airborne Pollen Concentration

Pollen count is the concerntration of pollen grains in cubic meter of air. Each spring, summer, and autumn, pollen grains are released from trees, weeds, and grasses. Despite the fact that their main goal is to fertilize different plants, many never arrive at their objectives. Instead, they enter human noses and throats and many people have an adverse effects due to the same. Our system act as helping hand for those people. One can get the predifted count for next two days and can plan their outings accordingly.
